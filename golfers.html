Biggest Weakness: Long Irons

The captain of Team USA. Anthony needs to dial in his iron play if he hopes to lead his team to victory. It's very possible he will face off against his counterpart Brian Busch and if he is not mentally ready for that matchup, things could go south fast. He has made some swing adjustments in the offseason so we will see if they pay off. USA! USA! USA!

Brian Busch

HCP: 16 / Shoots: Right

Biggest Weakness: Driver

The captain of Team Europe. Brian comes into this weekend as one of the best iron strikers on the field. Father Time will need to work on getting out of the tee box if he hopes to make a big difference for his team. He had a dream of getting this weekend set up and it's finally here. Will the pressure to perform be too much? Who knows.

Favorite Program: LOST

Tyler Barnes

HCP: 24 / Shoots: Right

Biggest Weakness: Driver

The doctor will see you now. Barnes hasn't been able to golf as much the past few years and that is the biggest question mark with his game right now because we know he has good speed on the greens. When it comes to getting off of the tee box, well it seems he can never finish. Very punny I know. Barnes could be a steal with his high HCP, but he still has to play well and that may be a challenge since he will be playing almost that same as last season. Expect Tyler to go in the mid rounds of the draft.

Favorite Band: Old Dominion

Taylor Bisnack

HCP: 10 / Shoots: Right

Biggest Weakness: The Slice

Taylor has gone through a number of injury woes so that will be something to keep an eye on throughout this weekend because nobody knows how his foot/ankle will hold up for four rounds. Other than that, this is a golfer that loves to compete. He prefers to golf when temps are near freezing, so warm weather could harm his game. This is one of this weekend's top golfers.
